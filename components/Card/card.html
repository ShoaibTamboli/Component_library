Card
A card is a flexible and extensible content container. It includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors, and powerful display options.

Text only card
Text only card is the one that contains a title, a descriptive text. It is also known as simple card. To implement use the Card Component, card-title, and card-text inside a div with class card.

Vertical Card
Vertical Card is the one that contains an Image, Title & Text one below another. To use the Card Component, put card-image, card-title and card-text, inside a div with class card.
